Daily feeding programs

Meal served 4.3 lakhs and counting

Encouraging every kid via the "Share A smile" project, which is part of the daily feeding program
The nation as a whole suffers from a serious problem with child malnutrition, which feeds the cycle of poverty, slow economic progress, and health issues. Poor mother health and lack of food throughout a kid's early years of life are the main causes of India's high child death rate. One kid in three is underweight, according to the 2021 National Family Health Survey (NFHS)-5 India report.

Even while India is still making progress in many areas, child malnutrition and hunger continue to be among the most urgent problems. A youngster who is hungry won't be able to provide their best effort, which will result in uneven possibilities for them. Long-term hunger causes malnutrition in children, which has negative effects on their physical, mental, and social development.

Share A smile helps kids eat their meals every day. Ensuring that every child receives the proper nourishment during their formative years is the aim in order to promote their complete development.
